Processing in Power Automate >5000 rows

What we want to achieve? Power Automate from Microsoft is a powerful automation tool. It allows you to really simplify your work by creating a flow that will process large amounts of data. Thanks to the schedule option, all the work can be done at night, and in the morning, when you sit down to work, you have the finished result. The problem is that right from the start, we encounter a significant limitation that, at first glance, may completely disqualify the further use of Power Automate—the limit on the amount of data we can process.
